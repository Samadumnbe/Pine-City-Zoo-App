GEMSBOK

The gemsbok or gemsbuck (Oryx gazella) is a large antelope in the Oryx genus .It is native to the arid regions of Southern Africa such as the kalahari Desert . Some authorities formely included the East African oryx as a subspecies. The gemsbok is depicted on the coat of arms of Namibia, where the current population of the species is estimated at 373,000 individuals.
